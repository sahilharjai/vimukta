About US

The New Way to Success

The institute was named VIMUKTAwith the vision to impart education in an innovative and effective manner, where the students are independent to express their thoughts and develop their own way of studying so that minimum efforts produce extraordinary results and thus the phenomenon of VIMUKTA is achieved.

With this, the institute is on its path of 'Making Success a habit'

Our goal at vimukta Classes (VC) is to provide knowledge and guidance and thereby create an environment that not only guides students to the path of success, but also inspires them to recognize and explore their own potential. All Courses at VC are based on the time tested teaching methodology which has been perfected and which has produced unmatched results in IITJEE consistently.

Vimukta Classes is an initiative to create scholars under its banner. We at Vimukta Classes will try to give the complete package of Overall Academic Development such as increasing students IQ level, develop the thinking ability of the students, counselling for better career options after 10+2, bringing the 100% of all the students and last but not the least preparing students for selection in India's top most Engineering Colleges with top ranks.

We at Vimukta Classes have pool of dedicated and experienced full time faculties whose vision and mission are same.

All faculties are well experienced and have proven track records of creating IITians, they know how to brings the best from any student.
