About US

VIMUKTA CONSTITUTES

Value System
At Vimukta Classes we retain the value of education with truth, transparency and commitment. Our value system revolves around our honest hard work. Whatever we commit we do. We present to you what we actually are. We believe in disciplined Execution of Course Planning & Syllabus. We have limited strength in our batches. At Vimukta Classes our counsellors will be ready to help you out from the stress of time table management at home, school and coaching management etc. We limit our batch sizes. The faculty will ensure the syllabus compliance of students in school, including school unit tests/half yearly exams and final exams. Our faculty is always willing to clear doubts of students even individually if required

Innovative programs and Concepts
Vimukta Classes is known for Innovations in Academic Excellence. We at Vimukta Classes believe that in current scenario competition is very tough, the expectation level of parents has increased, they want nothing less than best. All parents are trying to provide the best possible education for their child to excel. Knowing the fact that we cannot have more than 24 hours in a day, we developed few mechanism which are the true example of innovations. These innovations are practiced in our classes. We personally ensures about the progress of each and every student. We believe that teaching is of no use without frequent tests and tests are of no meaning without its thorough discussion, evaluation and identifying the week areas. We designed our curriculum strictly on the basis of the Schools. Our syllabus for school going students is strictly based on their school flow.

Micro analysing performance
We ensure that students spend quality time on learning and understanding of course rather than generally being distracted. Parents are regularly updated on the child’s progress through monthly progress report. The student’s performance and attendance is discussed to keep parents on the same page. We conduct personality development sessions to improve communication and presentation skills which are equally important as academic skills.

Unparallel Growth
Ever growing trust of students & parents as a ‘sincere’ institute is the sole reason of students and our growth. The Director of the Vimukta Classes has proven track record in the field of coaching Industry since year 2010. Under his nourishing hands the institute picked up strength and gained heights of excellence. At Vimukta Classes student satisfaction is guaranteed. We aim to meet society's needs for tomorrow. It is our constant and unfailing endeavour to tap the intellectual and creative potential of our children. We encourage our students into forming habits of responsibility, fortitude, a sweetness of disposition and self-discipline so that they emerge with a good a sense of social responsibility and commitment to the integrity of our nation.

Knowledge-based Organization

Application-based, conceptual & Effective Classroom Teaching is our prime motive. We follow interactive approach to promote & solve/clear the doubts/concepts.

Technology driven learning
We use cutting edge technology, to fulfil the needs of modern day student by creating comprehensive study material. Our faculty members are highly qualified and efficient with flawless concept clarity vision and efficient learning skills.  Our content is specially designed to explain every topic through simplified concepts and examples. Scoring higher marks in exams requires application-based learning, with a strong grasp of the fundamentals. Memorizing answers will be replaced by an understanding of concepts so that students perform well academically, and are also prepared for their long term career goals.
